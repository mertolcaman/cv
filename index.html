ABOUT ME

I earned my BSc in Mechanical Engineering after studying at multiple institutions through exchange programs - including Yıldız Technical University (Farabi) and Silesian University of Technology in Poland (Erasmus). These experiences broadened my perspective both academically and culturally.

I recently completed my MSc in Data Science at the University of Sussex, where I focused my dissertation on analyzing social media sentiment in cryptocurrency markets. I combined large language models (LLMs) for sentiment analysis with LSTM models to forecast price trends, giving me hands-on experience with time series data and NLP.

I'm a highly motivated learner with a strong passion for building and experimenting with intelligent systems. I enjoy tackling complex problems, learning new tools, and developing innovative solutions - especially at the intersection of AI, data, and full-stack development.

Outside of technology, I enjoy playing guitar and have performed in several events and competitions during my school years. This creative outlet helps me maintain balance while continuing to grow in my technical journey.
